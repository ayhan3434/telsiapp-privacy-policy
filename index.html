TelsiApp Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 05, 2026

TelsiApp ("we", "us", or "our") operates a walky-talky mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address (for registration)
  • Google account information (if you sign in with Google)
  • Voice data (transmitted in real-time during push-to-talk, not stored)
  • Location data (only when emergency mode is activated by the user)
  • Profile photos (uploaded by the user)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the TelsiApp service
  • To enable real-time voice communication between users
  • To send push notifications for channel activity
  • To share location during emergency alerts

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage)
  • Agora.io (real-time voice communication)
  • Google Maps (location display)

Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time from within the app. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed from our systems.

Permissions

  • Microphone: Required for push-to-talk voice communication
  • Location: Used only when emergency mode is activated
  • Camera/Storage: Used for profile photo upload
  • Notifications: Used for channel activity alerts
